Capstone Project

Capstone Project 1 and 2 (Thesis)

Mosquinator: A Community-Based Reporting Mobile and Web Application on Mosquito Borne-Diseases Awareness for Pasig Anti Dengue Task Force

This study aims to develop a community-oriented mobile application that will advocate a practical approach to providing a platform for reporting concerns and the exchange of information between the Pasig Dengue Task Force and the residents of Pasig City about dengue-related problems or incidents, as well as promoting awareness and knowledge regarding mosquito-borne diseases for the residents.

This project was developed by our team, Innovation Geeks, which consists of a four-member group of IT students specializing in Mobile and Web Applications under the guidance of Prof. Leonila B. Valdez, a faculty member of the College of Computing and Information Technologies at the National University of Manila.
